Why was the data set created?

Used by Trip Planning to define the route of the Benson Line Streetcar; which does not operate on city streets; but on its own rail right-of-way. The route footprint is required to calculate arrival & departure times at individual streetcar stations in order to quote them to users of the Trip Planning System and to control other aspects of trip plans such as walking directions and distances.
