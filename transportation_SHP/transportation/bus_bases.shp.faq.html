What does this data set describe?

Title: Metro Bus Bases in King County
Abstract:
The garage or depot for storage; fueling; and/or maintaince of buses when not in service. Transit operators are assigned to and report to work at a base. Buses are also assigned to a base.
Supplemental_Information: None
