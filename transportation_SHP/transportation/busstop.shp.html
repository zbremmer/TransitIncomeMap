Description:
Abstract:
On-street location where transit vehicles stop to pick-up and discharge passengers. It has a sign and basic service information; sometimes also a shelter with benches.
Purpose:
Used by trip planning application to plan customer itinerary and show riders where to board or alight. Facility planning uses data to analyze the bus stop usage. The data determines whether stops should be added; relocated or removed. Service planning uses the data to determine which areas need more service or a change of service.
